01 / About Us

More than a sound system — a host for your night.

Encore Echoes Entertainment (E.E.E.) started with one simple belief: every event deserves someone who cares as much about the night going right as the host does. Based in Palmetto, Florida, and proudly women-owned, we bring professional-grade sound and lighting together with something you can't buy off a gear list — real hospitality.

Weddings, private parties, corporate mixers, or a full karaoke night — our team reads the room and builds the soundtrack around your crowd, keeping the energy exactly where it needs to be. Nothing is on autopilot.

What sets us apart isn't only the equipment. It's that our owner is personally involved in setting the tone for the night — the reason our karaoke nights in particular have built such a loyal following. And with booking open 24 hours a day, locking in your date is never a hassle.
